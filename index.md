<section class="home-intro-copy" aria-label="Introduction">
  <div class="home-intro-main">
    <p>Welcome to my personal website! My name is Chendi Lin (林晨笛), and I currently work as a Senior Autonomous Vehicles Behavior Planning Engineer at NVIDIA. Previously, I led the Trajectory Selection component at AutoX and worked on behavior planning for Waymo's trucking team as a Software Engineer. I graduated as a Master's student in Robotics at Carnegie Mellon University, and I obtained my Bachelor's degree in Engineering Mechanics at University of Illinois, Urbana-Champaign. Traveling, sports, and writing are the activities that take up most of my free time. I am also on my way to becoming an amateur playwright.</p>
